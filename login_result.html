Frequently asked questions

Is Free Forever really free?

Yes. No credit card required. No time limit. You can build and publish your storefront completely free.

Can I upgrade later?

Absolutely. Upgrade anytime from your dashboard. Your data, products, and settings stay exactly as they are.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade whenever you need. Changes take effect immediately.

Do agencies manage multiple creators?

Yes. Agency plans include a multi-tenant dashboard. Switch between creator workspaces without logging out.

Do you charge transaction fees?

CreatorStore does not charge per-transaction fees on paid plans. Your revenue is yours.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. Creator Pro, Elite, and all Agency plans include custom domain support with free SSL.
